Hotline Miami is a fast-paced top-down action game set in a neon-soaked 1980s Miami. Players take on the role of a masked hitman, executing brutal missions against the criminal underworld while navigating a surreal, violent, and psychedelic world.

FAITH is a game about a young exorcist confronting demonic forces and uncovering dark secrets in a haunted world. Players explore eerie locations, solve puzzles, and face terrifying supernatural entities, all while experiencing a retro-inspired, psychological horror atmosphere.

I Have No Mouth, and I Must Scream is a game about five survivors trapped and tormented by a sadistic supercomputer named AM after humanity has been wiped out. Players navigate each character’s personal nightmare, making choices to survive while facing psychological horror, moral dilemmas, and extreme suffering.

Cry of Fear is a game about a young man trapped in a nightmarish city after a car accident. Players must explore dark streets, avoid and fight horrifying monsters, solve puzzles, and uncover the truth behind his terrifying visions. It’s a psychological horror experience focused on survival, fear, and mystery.

Stray is a game about a stray cat navigating a futuristic, neon-lit city inhabited by robots. Players explore rooftops, solve environmental puzzles, and uncover the mysteries of this abandoned world while experiencing life from a cat’s perspective.
